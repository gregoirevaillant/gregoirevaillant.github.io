Membership

To become a member simply print and complete the attached Membership Application form and post it with your payment. Membership fees are $30 single and $50 family. Each member will receive :

  • 3 Mallee Stump newsletters per year
  • Free access to all library records held by the WDFHG

Non-Members We able to carry our research for non-members.

  • An initial, up-front, non-refundable search fee of $30 per family name, covers the first hours search of our library resources, local newspapers and family files. This fee will cover up to 10 photocopies and postage if necessary, or results can be emailed.
  • You will be advised if there is any additional information available over and above the initial fee.
  • The initial request will need to be emailed or posted letter, giving us as much information and copies of certificates etc. as is relevant to the request.
  • Please note that we are unable to carry out our research until payment is received.
Please also enclose a completed Research Request Form ( which can be printed from this site), a cheque for $30 and a stamped self addressed D6 envelope.

Services

Our Library Records Room The records rooms are open each second and fourth Saturday of the month from 2 pm. till 4 pm. The rooms can be opened at other times by appointment and visitors are always welcome.

GRAVE PHOTOGRAPHS Photographs of graves and headstones in local cemeteries can be provided for $5 per photograph. Please contact the research officer.

RESEARCH OFFICER - Pam Butcher Email: genealogy@wyalongfamilyhistory.com

RESEARCH MAY TAKE 3 TO 6 WEEKS Depending on the number of enquiries received and the availability of the Research Team.All research will be dealt with by order of date received, promptly and as accurately as possible. A fee of $10 is requested to use the resources at the Family History Group rooms if non members wish to do their own research.

Monthly Meetings

Our genealogy meetings for members are held :

  • On the first Saturday of each month at 1:30 p.m.
Please note that there are no meetings held in January.

Member Meeting Location The Wyalong District Family History Group hold it's monthly meetings either in the Bland Centre hall or outside at a historical location.

After the meeting closes, members are always encouraged to stay and enjoy a cup of tea and a chat.

Annual General Meeting These meetings are held on the first Saturday in August. A copy of the minutes of these meeting are available to members.
